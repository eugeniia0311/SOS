What is SOS?

Social Battle
Royale Game

Each round you and 15 other contestants comete to escape a deadly island filled with monsters. The trick is: three people can survive. Will you run solo or form friendships with others to escape?
Making right decisions could be the difference between life and death.

Why so serious?

Features

  • Survive at all costs

    You have 30 minutes to find a relic, signal for extraction, and grab one of three spots on the rescue chopper.

  • Create allies and enemies

  • Impress the audience
